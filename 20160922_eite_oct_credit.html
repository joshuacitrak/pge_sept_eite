A Message from the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC)*
This October you will receive a credit as a line item on your electric bill called “CA Industry Assistance” from the State of California. If you maintain your eligibility, you will continue receiving this credit each April through 2020. The October credit is the first payout and retroactively covers the period of 2013-2016.**

The CA Industry Assistance Credit is one of many programs resulting from landmark legislation called the Global Warming Solutions Act of 2006. Together, these programs are cutting pollution, creating jobs, and investing in cleaner energy and transportation.

The CPUC calculates the credit amounts for each facility using formulas which reward businesses that have taken early action to reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.

Your CA Industry Assistance Credit is designed to help compensate you for a portion of the GHG emissions costs associated with the electricity you buy. You can use the credit however you choose, but you can save money by investing in energy efficiency, including more efficient lighting, heating, and cooling. Find more information at www.EnergyUpgradeCA.org/credit.

Please note: If your credit exceeds your monthly bill balance, you may leave the credit on your bill to use towards future energy charges or you may contact your utility to request a check for any remaining bill credit. If you were previously receiving the small business California Climate Credit, you will no longer receive the small business California Climate Credit and will now receive the annual CA Industry Assistance Credit.

For additional information, visit www.cpuc.ca.gov/industryassistance.


* The CPUC regulates privately owned electric companies and serves the public interest by protecting consumers and ensuring the provision of safe, reliable utility service and infrastructure at reasonable rates, with a commitment to environmental enhancement and a healthy California economy. For more information about our work, contact us at: news@cpuc.ca.gov, 1-800-253-0500, or visit www.cpuc.ca.gov
** Billing periods vary by customer and may not always coincide with a calendar month. If you don’t see a CA Industry Assistance Credit in the bill that arrives in October, it will appear in the bill you receive in November. Likewise, if you don’t see a CA Industry Assistance Credit in the bill that arrives in April, it will appear in the bill you receive in May.
